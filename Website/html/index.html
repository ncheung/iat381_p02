Vinyl Bros.

Vinyl Bros. was established in 1996 by cofounders Avi Shack and Wes Kuitenbrouwer. Originally a Skateboard/Clothing/Lifestyle store, Beat Street quickly evolved into a record store. A record mecca for DJs in the 90s/2000s, Vinyl Bros. moved locations 4 times, landing in the current location on Hastings Street in 2004. vinylbros.ca went online in 2002, and has undergone 4 major changes, the most recent in March 2012.
